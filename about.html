Sunny is a coder who ponders much
about the intersection of technology, art,
ethics, and her role in a gloablized world.
Sunny received her bachelors in Women and
Gender studies from San Francisco State
University in 2019. Recieving her bachelors
awakened Sunny to the endless paths in which
we may create a more equitable world. Sunny
belives creating inclusive accessible technology
is one such path. Sunny is skilled in Python, SQL,
Flask, HTML, CSS, Javascript and
is always learning more!

Sunny's hobbies include cooking, exercising,
making music, gardening, and spending time
with loved ones. She is passionate about tech
finanacial literacy, spreading kindness, and
is dedicated to being a life-long learner.
Sunny aspires to be the best version of
herself in all she does, whether that is
as a software engineer, friend or human.
She uses her values of positivity, growth,
teamwork, precision, empathy, and patience
as guideposts in life
