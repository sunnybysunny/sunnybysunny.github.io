Sunny is skilled in Python3, Javascript (ES6), Flask,
SQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript and more. Sunny highly
values collaboration, feedback, and creativity
when problem solving

making music, reading, gardening, and spending time
with loved ones. She is passionate about tech

a life-long learner. Sunny aspires to be the
best version of herself in all she does, whether
that is as a software engineer, friend or human.
She uses her values of positivity, growth,
teamwork, precision, empathy, creativity,
and patience as guideposts in life
