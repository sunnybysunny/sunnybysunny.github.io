My journey into software development began in early 2022,
I started learning Python, and like many developers my first lines of code were print("Hello world").
However my interest in software and technology was sparked long before 2022.

In 2019 I received my bachelors in Women and Gender studies, the curriculum for this degree cultivated
my critical thinking, problem solving, as well as research and analysis skills, but most importantly
it inspired me to work to create a more equitable world. I learned technology is a great tool for improving
quality of life, whether that is through creating VR/AR environments for therapy, to designing robotic prosthesis
to help people with disabilites; the potential to help others through tech is limitless and makes me excited to be in this field!

In 2023 I recieved my certification for completing the Ada Developers Academy core curriculum in software engineering and
computer science fundamentals. I have experience with Python, Javascript, Flask, React, and I'm always learning more!
In my work I highly value collaboration, creativity, learning, attention to detail, and approaching problem solving
from multiple perspectives. I dream of working with a team that aspires to create technology that is innovative, accessible,
and improves the quality of life for living beings.
